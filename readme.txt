Adds Canada Post shipping rates to WooCommerce 
== Description ==
Canada Post Shipping For WooCommerce By CanShip is a plugin that adds support for Canada Post shipping to WooCommerce. After installing the plugin your customers will be offered the ability to choose Canada Post as a shipping method during the checkout.
The plugin automatically checks the sizes of the items your customers are buying and calculates the shipping costs automatically.
